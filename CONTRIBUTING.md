# Contributing

This repo exists for the owner's personal workflow first. It is public because the app may still be useful to other people, but outside usefulness does not imply a public roadmap or support commitment.

Small bug reports, focused fixes, and documentation improvements are welcome.

Please keep these expectations in mind:

- Feature requests are fine, but they are not roadmap commitments.
- Avoid broad refactors or architectural rewrites without discussing them first.
- Preserve the app's safety-first behavior around Apple Photos.
- Do not add background network behavior, telemetry, or direct deletion flows.
- Prefer small, reviewable changes over large speculative ones.
